Our Mission & Vision

Empowerment of Nigerian children through the provision of quality education. Provision of free health care services to rural dwellers. Empowerment of unemployed persons with vocational and technical skills to enable them become self employed. Promotion of agricultural development and social responsibility. Provision of welfare services to the disadvantaged.

About Us

The Chidi Anyaegbu Foundation founded by Chief Dr. Chidi Anyaegbu in the year 2016 is a duly registered non-governmental organization. It is an organization established to assist the less privileged in a country where most of the citizens continue to suffer untold hardship.

The foundation was formed due to the fact that the founder was not born with a silver spoon in his mouth. This fact prevented him from furthering his education as a child. Hence, he took to apprenticeship after his primary school education.

Having passed through some difficult times during his youth and now blessed by God, he has vowed to help children and young people especially those from poor family backgrounds. He intends to help them acquire quality education and other social benefits.

Our Achievements

  • The construction of an ultra modern building at the Department of Transportation Studies, Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka, Anambra State.
  • The sponsorship of an annual Free Medical Mission for the people of Amichi Community in Anambra State and beyond.
  • The establishment of a scholarship scheme for students in secondary and tertiary institutions in Amichi and its environs.

About The Founder

Chief Dr. Chidi Anyaegbu is a Nigerian businessman and transport mogul. He is the founder and owner of Chisco Group Limited and Havila Air. Chief Dr. Chidi Anyaegbu was born on September 13, 1956 in Amichi, Nnewi South Local Government Area of Anambra State.

EDUCATIONAL BACKGROUND

Chief Dr. Chidi Anyaegbu obtained his First School Leaving Certificate from St. Andrew`s Primary School, Amichi, Nnewi in 1971. He attended several Management and Entrepreneurial courses in France, USA, Brazil, Germany, and the United Kingdom. In 1996, Chidi Anyaegbu was conferred with a Doctorate Degree in Business Administration (Honoris Causa) by Penn State University, USA.

CAREER AND BUSINESS

Chidi Anyaegbu is the founder and CEO of Chisco Group; a conglomerate sprung across transportation, oil and gas, finance, hospitality, real estate, import and export. He ventured into business in 1975 after he was given One Hundred and Twenty Naira (#120.00) by his uncle Daniel Ofodum after 4 years apprenticeship. He sold spare parts at the spare parts market at Ebute Metta in Lagos. In 1981, he started Chisco Transport Nigeria Ltd and subsequently diversified into oil and gas, hospitality, courier, real estate and other companies.

Chisco Group has subsidiaries which include:

  • Chisco Transport Nigeria Limited
  • CTN Express Ltd.
  • Villa Angelia Guest House
  • Angel Park Palace Hotel
  • Havillah Oil
  • Base Bureau De Change
  • Chisco Haulage and Keenline Investment
  • Havillah Airways
