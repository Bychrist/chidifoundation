WHAT WE DO

To help those whose lives and livelihoods are shattered by conflicts and disasters survive, recover, and gain control of their future.

Medical & Health Missions

Our medical and health missions fund doctors, nurses, and support staff to provide surgical and medical care to underserved communities around the world

Education

We executes funding in areas of highest need for education. We do this via credible partners and data analytics.

Entrepreneur - Start, run and grow your business.

The Dr. Chidi Anyaegbu Foundation inspire the next generation of Entrepreneurs by providing them with the funding and skill set to overcome global business challenges.

Agriculture Support

Dr Chidi Anayaegbu Foundation leverages the power of collaboration between food and agricultural stakeholders to advance agriculture within communities in Nigeria and beyond

OUR TEAM

Our dedicated team are highly trained professionals. We carry out data analytics on projects and recomend the best process possible to achieve any projected goal
